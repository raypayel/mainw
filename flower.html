Famous Flowers of India

  • Rose
  • Lotus
  • Marigold
  • Jasmine

Rose

The rose is a universally loved flower, symbolizing love and beauty. In India, roses are used in various ceremonies and as decorative elements in gardens and homes.

Lotus

The lotus is a sacred flower in India, symbolizing purity and enlightenment. It is often associated with various deities in Hinduism and Buddhism and is renowned for its beautiful blooms that emerge from muddy waters.

Marigold

The marigold is a vibrant flower commonly used in Indian festivals and rituals. It comes in shades of orange and yellow and is known for its long-lasting blooms and distinct aroma.

Jasmine

Jasmine is celebrated for its sweet fragrance and delicate white or yellow flowers. It is widely used in making garlands, perfumes, and traditional medicine in India.
