Famous Animals of India

  • Indian Peacock
  • Bengal Tiger
  • Asian Elephant
  • Indian Cobra

Indian Peacock

The Indian peacock, known for its stunning iridescent feathers and elaborate courtship display, is the national bird of India. It is celebrated for its vibrant colors and graceful beauty.

Bengal Tiger

The Bengal tiger, native to India, is one of the largest and most powerful big cats. It is known for its distinctive orange coat with black stripes and is a symbol of strength and beauty.

Asian Elephant

The Asian elephant, found in India and other parts of Asia, is smaller than its African counterpart and has smaller ears. It plays a significant role in Indian culture and wildlife conservation.

Indian Cobra

The Indian cobra, known for its hood and potent venom, is a prominent snake species in India. It is often featured in Indian folklore and is a critical part of the ecosystem.
