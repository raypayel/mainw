Famous & Traditional Clothes of India

Lehenga Choli

The lehenga choli is a traditional outfit worn by women, especially during weddings and festivals. It consists of a long skirt (lehenga), a blouse (choli), and often a dupatta (scarf). The lehenga choli is known for its elaborate embroidery and vibrant colors.

Sari

The sari (or saree) is a traditional Indian garment worn by women. It consists of a long piece of cloth, usually around 6 to 9 yards, draped elegantly around the body. It is often paired with a blouse and a petticoat. Saris come in a wide variety of fabrics, colors, and styles, and are worn in different ways depending on the region.

Kurta and Pajama

The kurta is a long shirt or tunic, typically worn with pajama (loose-fitting trousers) or churidar (tighter-fitting trousers). This outfit is popular among men in India and is often worn casually or for traditional occasions. Kurtas can be adorned with embroidery or embellishments for festive events.

Salwar Kameez

The salwar kameez is a traditional outfit that includes a long tunic (kameez) paired with loose-fitting trousers (salwar) and often accompanied by a scarf (dupatta). It is a popular choice among women for both everyday wear and special occasions, and it can be found in various styles and fabrics.
